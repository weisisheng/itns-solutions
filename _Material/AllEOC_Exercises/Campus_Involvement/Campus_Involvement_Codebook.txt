This data is real data collected in an online survey of college students conducted by students in an upper-level seminar on happiness.  

Reported here is:
  Gender : Male or Female
  Gender_Code: 1 = female, 2 = male
   GPA: 4 point scale
  Commuter:  0 = resident, 1 = commuter
  SWB - Subjective Well Being - Average from 5-item SWB scale by Diener, scale from 1-5
  Campus Invovlement - Average of 8-item scale of campus involvement, scale from 1-5.
